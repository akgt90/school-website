About Us

Building strong minds, good values, and a bright future.

Prashanti Public School has been serving Kohlwin since 2004, providing quality education to children from nearby villages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education in a safe and disciplined environment where every child can learn, grow, and build a strong foundation for the future.

Our Vision

We aim to shape responsible students from Nursery to 10th with knowledge, confidence, good character, and respect for their community.

Core Values

What guides our school community

Excellence

We encourage students to do their best in academics, discipline, and daily life.

Integrity

We nurture honesty, accountability, and respect in everything we do.

Creativity

We support curiosity, active learning, and confidence in the classroom.

Community

We believe in partnership with families and service to nearby village communities.
