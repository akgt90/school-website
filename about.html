Prashanti Public School has been serving Kohlwin since 2004, providing quality education to children from nearby villages.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education in a safe and disciplined environment where every child can learn, grow, and build a strong foundation for the future. We believe every student deserves the opportunity to discover their potential.

🔭

Our Vision

We aim to shape responsible students from Nursery to 10th with knowledge, confidence, good character, and respect for their community. Our vision extends beyond textbooks — we build citizens who uplift their villages.
